# In a bind
A *man*, shocked by how his [buddy](https://www.rd.com/list/funniest-jokes-of-all-time/) is dressed, asks him, 
> “How long have you been wearing that bra?”

The **friend** replies, 
> “Ever since my wife found it in the glove compartment.”
